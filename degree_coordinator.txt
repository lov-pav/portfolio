Created a Degree Coordinator that allows students of University of Texas Permian Basin students to track which courses they need to take to meet graduation criteria. It allows students to track previous courses, current courses, grades, graduation eligiblity, and courses for the upcoming semester.
The application was designed using Figma and leverages Auth0's external API to handle security and login functionality and has significant database integration through use of MySQL.
The application supports user-role management, allowing students and administrators to login and make necessary changes.
